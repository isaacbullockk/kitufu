# AFCON 2027 Uganda — Pamoja Residences Platform Research

## Source Document Summary

### The "Adaptive Reuse" Strategy
- Convert empty buildings into "Pop-Up Fan Residences" (not permanent hotels)
- The Qatar Model: During the 2022 World Cup, Qatar converted thousands of empty apartments and office blocks into fan housing. They didn't "renovate"; they "kitted."
- The Kit Approach: Work with furniture suppliers to create a standardized "Room Kit" (bed, linens, side table, portable AC/fan, and a safe). This allows you to "furnish" an empty building in 72 hours and "de-kit" it after the tournament.
- Focus on the "Hoima Cluster": CAF is currently worried about a 10,000-room shortage there. Even a basic, clean, secure building in Hoima will be worth more than a 4-star hotel in Kampala during match weeks.

### Legal & Regulatory
- Uganda Tourism Board (UTB) is the sole authority that can "declare" a premise as a tourist facility.
- Must apply for "Temporary Tourism Accommodation" status.
- Minimum Standards — the "Four S's" of UTB/CAF compliance:
  1. Security: Perimeter fencing, 24/7 guards, and CCTV are non-negotiable for international fans.
  2. Sanitation: High-quality modular bathrooms or "containerized" toilet blocks.
  3. Safety: Fire escapes and extinguishers as per the 2019 National Building Code.
  4. Service: Provide a "Concierge Desk" in the lobby of each building to handle check-ins and transport.

### App Integration — "The Secret Collection"
- Category Name: "Pamoja Residences" or "AFCON Hubs"
- Bulk Booking for Groups: Perfect for Supporters' Clubs (e.g., 200 fans from Nigeria or Senegal). The app should allow a "Request to Buy Out Building" feature for large groups.
- "Last Mile" Logistics: Bundle with exclusive shuttles. The app should show: "Stay at the Pamoja Hub + Private Shuttle to Stadium included."

### Revenue Model
- Operate as a "Managed Market":
  - Master Lease: Take a master lease on the empty buildings for 3 months (June–August 2027).
  - The Spread: Pay the building owner a "vacancy fee" and keep the markup from the high-occupancy AFCON rates.
  - Pre-Sales: Use the app to sell "Season Passes" for a room for the entire duration of the group stages.

### Immediate Next Steps
1. Inventory Audit: Map every building by distance to Mandela National Stadium (Kampala) and Hoima City Stadium.
2. The "Sample Suite": Fully furnish one room in one of the buildings now. Use this to take high-quality photos for the app and to show the UTB/LOC that you can meet international standards.
3. Strategic Partnership: Approach the Uganda Hotel Owners Association (UHOA) and UTB.

### Risk Warning
- The biggest failure in Qatar was the "Building Site" feel—fans arriving to buildings with construction dust and poor AC.
- If you can guarantee cleanliness, security, and a working AC/Fan, you will have 100% occupancy the moment you go live.

## Context for Platform
- **Event**: Africa Cup of Nations (AFCON) 2027
- **Host**: Uganda (co-hosting with Tanzania)
- **Dates**: June – August 2027
- **Key Locations**: Kampala (Mandela National Stadium), Hoima (Hoima City Stadium)
- **Target Users**: International football fans, Supporters' Clubs, individual travelers
- **Key Differentiator**: "Pamoja Residences" — affordable, secure pop-up accommodations in converted buildings
